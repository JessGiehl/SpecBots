About Us

Spec Bots is a combat robot entertainment company operating in Orlando, FL. For the past two years we’ve been hosting Maker Faire Orlando, a large scale robot event that sees participants from across the country. Now we’re bringing the excitement and our passion for combat robotics to events across Central Florida!
